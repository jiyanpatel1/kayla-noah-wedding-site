Q + A

Are there any dress code restrictions?

Dress Code: Black Tie & Formal Please note that this event requires black tie and formal attire. Guests are kindly asked to avoid jeans, sneakers, or any casual clothing. Gentlemen are encouraged to wear tuxedos or dark suits, and ladies can opt for evening gowns or sophisticated cocktail dresses. We look forward to an elegant occasion where everyone is dressed to impress!

Are kids allowed?

While we truly love your little ones, due to the formal nature of our celebration we kindly request an adults-only evening. We appreciate your understanding and hope you enjoy a special night out celebrating with us.

Is the wedding indoor or outdoor?

The ceremony and cocktail hour will be held outdoors. Following cocktail hour, the reception will take place inside the ballroom, which is directly accessible from the outdoor cocktail area.

Will there be an open bar?

Yes! There will be wine, beer, seltzers & liquor available! With that being said, PLEASE BE RESPONSIBLE!
